Operations

When business is tough it is important the right infrastructure is in place and your processes are operating efficiently.

This section of TM ViSight Reports will assist you in managing your day-to-day operations and effective processes. Enabling you to manage staffing resources and facilities. 

Time of Day

This report identifies complete and incomplete calls by hour enabling you to identify peak calling times at particular times of day and highlighting incomplete calls. Incomplete calls can result in a lost sale or unhappy customers. This TM ViSight Report is designed to assist you to manage staffing resource and facilities (ie do you have sufficient telephone lines). To reduce unattended calls, action can be taken immediately from the most updated information.

Short Calls

Short calls are considered as ineffective calls as information cannnot be successfully communicated to your callers. The Short Calls report identifies calls received in range of 0-15 seconds, 16-30 seconds, 31-60 seconds, 1 minute to 2 minutes and 2 minutes and above. 

If the short calls are reduced and more calls are being effectively answered this will result in better customer service delivery, improve customer retention, and increase revenue with more calls being converted into sales.
